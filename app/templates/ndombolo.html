Ndombolo

Ndombolo is a dance from the Democratic Republic of the Congo, that is also popular in other African countries such as Kenya,Tanzania and Madagascar, who consider it as a music genre. This style of fast music, currently dominating dancefloors in central, eastern and western Africa and has since been performed by Dany Engobo, Awilo Longomba, Aurlus Mabele, Koffi Olomide and groups like Extra Musica and Wenge Musica, among others.
